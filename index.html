Hi There! I am Maribel Marín

I’m a Business analyst, UX designer and junior web development who currently resides in beautiful Barcelona, Spain. In 2009 I started working in the health area, specifically in telecare. Among my current skills are the study of processes and procedures, analyze strengths, improvement needs in tools and departments, find solutions, test and implement.

For this reason, I started to get interested in the user experience area, especially in user-centered design, and I graduated from Ironhack as a UX / UI designer in 2018 and in In June 2019 I finished my studies in Application Development with web technologies at the CIM-UPC Foundation. My goal now is to continue learning and progressing in the fields of UX and programming.
